廉洁主题--海报设计

画面一【人民公仆】画面围绕焦裕禄个人事迹展开，以复古报纸排版的方式，表达焦裕禄亲民爱民、艰苦奋斗、科学求实、迎难而上、无私奉献，焦裕禄的事迹和他留下的宝贵精神财富，激励着一代又一代中国人。如同最辛勤的园丁，癌症晚期仍坚持工作到生命最后一息，他带领兰考人民植树造林、改善土壤，给老百姓办实事的人永远不会被忘记。
画面二【燃灯校长】画面围绕张桂梅的个人事迹展开，以复古报纸排版的方式，表达张桂梅呕心沥血默默奉献，奋力撑开贫困山区女孩的庇护伞，以自己的灵魂唤醒千百个无措的灵魂，萤烛末光亦能照亮他人。
画面三【中国市长】画面围绕耿彦波个人事迹展开，以复古报纸排版的方式，表达耿彦波的一生正气两袖清风，一尘不染克己奉公。他的事迹使人看完肃然起敬，他的目光如同炬火，燃尽世间一切混沌。

Picture 1 [People's Servant] focuses on Jiao Yulu's personal story. It uses retro newspaper typeset to express Jiao's love for the people, hard work, science and truth, facing difficulties and selfless dedication. Jiao's story and the precious spiritual wealth he left behind have inspired generations of Chinese people. Like the most diligent gardener, cancer terminal still insist on working until the last breath, he led the Lankao people to plant trees, improve the soil, to do practical things for the people will never be forgotten.
picture 2 is about Zhang Guimei's personal story. In the way of retro newspaper composition, it expresses Zhang Guimei's painstaking and silent dedication, striving to open the shelter umbrella of girls in poor mountainous areas, waking up hundreds of souls at a loss with her own soul, and illuminating others with fireflies.
Picture 3 [Chinese Mayor] The picture focuses on Geng Yanbo's personal deeds. It expresses Geng Yanbo's life integrity and integrity in the way of retro newspaper typeset. His deeds made people look at the end of awe, his eyes like a fire, burning all the chaos in the world.
